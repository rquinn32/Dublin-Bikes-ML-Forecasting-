Getting started with Dublin Bikes

How to use Dublin Bikes

Everything you need to know to find a station, plan your trip, rent a bike, return it, and share your experience.

Step 1

Find your station

Locate the best station for your journey.

  • Use the interactive map on the homepage
  • Search for a station by name
  • Check how many bikes are available
  • Plan ahead with predicted bike availability
Step 2

Plan your journey

Choose the best route for you.

  • Use the Route Planner on the map page
  • Click “Start Route” to begin
  • Select your starting point and destination
  • Follow the directions to the nearest station, then continue to your destination
Step 3

Rent your bike

Unlock a bike and start your ride.

  • Go to your chosen station
  • Select an available bike
  • Pay at the station when renting your bike
  • See the Pricing page for full details
  • Start your ride and enjoy the city
Step 4

Return your bike

Dock your bike safely at any available station.

  • Find a nearby return station on the map
  • Check for available docking spaces
  • Push the bike securely into the dock
  • Make sure the return is confirmed
Step 5

Leave a review

Tell us about your experience using Dublin Bikes.

  • Scroll to the review section on the homepage
  • Choose your rating
  • Add comments about your trip
  • Submit your review to help other users

Discover Dublin

Explore the City

Explore the City

Discover Dublin in the most flexible and fun way with DublinBikes. Glide through the lively streets, from the historic charm of Trinity College Dublin to the vibrant atmosphere of Temple Bar, all at your own pace. Whether you're commuting, sightseeing, or just enjoying a spontaneous ride along the River Liffey, DublinBikes makes it easy to experience the city up close. Skip the traffic, embrace the fresh air, and uncover hidden gems around every corner—your Dublin adventure starts with a single pedal.

Temple Bar District

Temple Bar District

Step into the heart of Dublin’s cultural scene in the iconic Temple Bar district. Known for its cobbled streets, colourful buildings, and lively atmosphere, this area is a must-visit for music, art, and nightlife lovers. Park your DublinBike nearby and wander through buzzing pubs, street performances, and unique galleries, or soak up the energy with a quick coffee or bite to eat. Whether day or night, Temple Bar offers an unforgettable slice of Dublin’s spirit just a short ride away.

Easy Access Stations

Easy Access Stations

Getting around Dublin has never been simpler, thanks to DublinBikes’ network of conveniently located stations across the city. From transport hubs like Heuston Station and Connolly Station to popular spots such as St Stephen's Green, you’re never far from your next ride. With easy pick-up and drop-off points, you can plan your journey with total flexibility—perfect for commuting, exploring, or making quick stops along the way. Just grab a bike and go wherever the city takes you.

Scenic Routes

Scenic Routes

Experience Dublin at its most beautiful by exploring its scenic cycling routes with DublinBikes. Cruise along the River Liffey for stunning waterfront views, or take a relaxing ride through the lush greenery of Phoenix Park, one of Europe’s largest city parks. For a coastal escape, follow the path out to Clontarf Promenade and enjoy fresh sea air and panoramic views of Dublin Bay. Whether you’re after nature, cityscapes, or a bit of both, these routes offer the perfect backdrop for a memorable ride.
